Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

Minimalist Launcher ("we", "our", or "the app") is a minimalist launcher application. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data.
  • All user preferences such as wallpaper selection, favorites, hidden apps, and settings are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

2. Microphone & Voice Recognition

  • The app uses the device microphone only when you manually activate the voice unlock feature.
  • The microphone is used temporarily to recognize spoken app names.
  • Audio data is not recorded, stored, transmitted, or shared.
  • Voice processing is handled by the device or operating system's speech recognition service.
  • The microphone is never accessed in the background.

3. App Usage Access Permission

  • If you enable smart features such as automatic hiding of unused apps, the app may request Usage Access permission.
  • Usage data is processed only on your device.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit app usage information.
  • This data is used only to improve launcher functionality (e.g., hiding unused apps).
  • Granting this permission is optional.

4. Installed Apps Information

  • The app accesses the list of installed applications on your device in order to display and launch apps as part of the launcher functionality.
  • This information remains on your device and is not transmitted or shared.

5. Qur'an Content

  • The app displays Qur'an verses for inspiration and reading purposes.
  • Qur'an text is stored locally for offline use.
  • Translation sources are credited in the app's Credits section.
  • We do not track or collect reading activity.

6. In-App Purchases

  • The app may offer optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features.
  • Payments are processed securely by Google Play.
  • We do not store or process payment information.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services (for purchases and system integration)
  • Android speech recognition services

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Data Security

All user data and settings are stored locally on the device. We take reasonable steps to protect the app from unauthorized access.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
