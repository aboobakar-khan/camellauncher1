## üåê Quick Setup with GitHub Pages

### Step 1: Enable GitHub Pages
1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/aheteshamq25/minimalist_app
2. Click on **Settings** tab
3. Scroll down to **Pages** in the left sidebar
4. Under **Source**, select **main** branch
5. Click **Save**

### Step 2: Wait a few minutes
- GitHub will build your site (usually takes 1-5 minutes)
- You'll see a green banner with your URL

### Step 3: Get Your Privacy Policy URL
Your privacy policy will be available at:
```
https://aheteshamq25.github.io/minimalist_app/privacy_policy.html
```

### Step 4: Use in Play Store
- Copy this URL
- Paste it in the "Privacy Policy" field when submitting to Google Play Store
